Set in a world where something unseen is beginning to rot the very fabric of life, Crimson Aether is a story of brutal survival, hidden truths, and the fragile line between protection and control.

At the heart of the world lies Aether — the unseen force that sustains life, fuels magic, and forges kingdoms. But something has begun to change. A distorted form, known as Crimson Aether, is spreading, corrupting the land, twisting what it touches and leaving ruin in its wake.

In the city of Asteria, people have lived beneath the quiet, enduring protection of the sacred relic known as the Aetherheart. The streets are safe. The walls hold. Rival kingdoms kept at bay. Until one night, everything changes.

When the Aetherheart is stolen, the city's stability begins to falter in ways no one fully understands. The truth is kept hidden. Panic is contained. And a small group is gathered to go beyond the city's reach to retrieve it — before anyone realizes what's been lost.

Among them is Cass Severin, a reserved but capable adventurer. Drawn into a mission he doesn't fully trust, he finds himself traveling among strangers whose motives are far from clear — none more so than Claire Ashwood, whose confidence masks something deeper. What begins as a single mission quickly grows into something far larger, spanning fractured kingdoms, forgotten powers, and a world slowly unraveling. From fortified cities in the sky to distant lands shaped by Aether itself, their journey will uncover the truth behind the spreading Crimson Aether and the forces that seek to control it.
